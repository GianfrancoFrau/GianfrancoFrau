Gianfranco Frau

Web Developer

Passionate about web development, I started my career in 2014 by contributing to the development and production release of the products of the Cagliari-based startup Glaamy. In this experience I worked on the backend and web frontend, on mobile applications for iOS and Android with Titanium Appcelerator technology. The next experience, which is the current one, is in Vivocha where I deal with the frontend development both for the company product and for the internal projects of Covisian, the parent company.
